About Chi Local Shows / FAQ

What is this?

A page that pulls together upcoming local band shows from a handful of Chicago venues, so you don't have to check a dozen venue websites and Instagram feeds one by one.

Why did you make this?

Personal itch — I wanted an easy way to see what small, DIY-leaning shows were coming up around Chicago without checking every venue separately. It's a spare-time hobby project.

How does it work?

Once a week (Sunday mornings), an automated job visits each venue's events page, reads it, and pulls out show listings — bands, date, time, ticket link. For each band found, it searches for that band's Spotify, Instagram, and Bandcamp pages, starting with a general web search and falling back to Spotify's own artist search API, and runs some basic sanity checks (name-similarity matching) before accepting a match — so a small local band doesn't get mixed up with an unrelated famous artist who happens to share a name.

Which venues are included? Can more be added?

The initial list is just venues I personally follow and wanted to surface smaller/local acts from — it's not meant to be exhaustive. Venues are added via a simple config file, so adding more is easy. If there's a venue you'd like to see included, email me below.

Does this cover comedy, karaoke, DJ nights, etc.?

No — the goal is specifically to surface live bands, especially at smaller venues, not comedy, karaoke, or DJ sets.

Something's wrong about my band or my show — how do I fix it?

Right now this is a static website with no login or edit form, so there's no self-serve way for bands to correct their own info yet. Email the address below and it'll get fixed by hand. Building a real submit-a-correction flow would take real additional work to bolt onto a static site — I'm open to it if there's enough interest.

I'm a developer and have ideas.

Feedback, issues, and pull requests are welcome — github.com/colton-lapp/chi-local-shows.

A better version of this already exists — what should I do?

Please tell me! I'd rather point people at something better than duplicate effort — email me below and I'll take this site down.

Where is this hosted? Could it move?

Free, on my personal GitHub account via GitHub Pages. If there's real interest from the local music community, moving to a dedicated domain is on the table.
